☕️ Today’s “egg nog latte” recipe:

  • Sprinkle ginger, cardamom, clove, nutmeg in coffee mug
  • Crag 1 egg into coffee mug and mix up with a fork
  • Brew a pourover into mug
  • Add a little heavy cream and some allulose
  • Stir again with that fork

Enjoy!
